## Recent News

### 2026-04-03 — MoU with B2EN for Stablecoin & AI (edaily)

apM Members signed an MoU with B2EN (AI/Big Data firm) to introduce stablecoin-based payment infrastructure in the Dongdaemun fashion wholesale district. Key initiatives:

- Stablecoin payment infra for apM shopping malls
- AI analysis of payment data for merchant marketing insights
- Joint R&D on apM Members platform data and AI models
- Global fashion distribution data utilization

B2EN previously partnered with Binance Pay for digital asset payment standards targeting foreign tourists. Dongdaemun — with 80%+ international visitors — will serve as the core testbed.

Source: https://n.news.naver.com/mnews/article/018/0006249193

### 2026-02-26 — SentBe e-Voucher Supply Contract (ZDNet Korea)

apM Members signed an e-voucher supply contract with SentBe (Singapore-based global fintech with MPI license). SentBe will resell apM e-vouchers to overseas buyers using its stablecoin off-ramp infrastructure, reducing processing time and fees vs. traditional methods. Pilot testing planned for Q1, with phased service expansion to follow.

Source: https://n.news.naver.com/article/092/0002411958
